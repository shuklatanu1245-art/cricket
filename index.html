Tournament Details

Format

T20 format played with premium quality Leather ball. Knockouts followed by group stages.

Prize Pool

Winning team takes home $5,000 + Trophy. Exciting awards for Man of the Series and Best Bowler/Batsman.

Eligibility

Open to all amateur clubs and individuals above 16 years of age. Professional players restricted.

Rules & Guidelines

Each team must register a squad of exactly 15 players. Playing 11 must be declared before the toss. No substitutions allowed once the match starts, except for concussion or injury as per ICC guidelines.
Teams must report 45 minutes before scheduled start time. A delay of more than 15 minutes will result in a penalty of 1 over per 5 minutes. Delay beyond 30 minutes results in a walkover.
A valid Government ID must be uploaded during registration. Players without matching ID proof on match day will not be allowed to play.
